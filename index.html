Discover Diptyque's new collection

Les Essences De diptyque

Bringing us back to nature, Diptyque’s new collection of perfumes uses the scents of Mother Earth to spark our imagination and fuel our inner creativity. The perfumes not only incites our scent, but allows us to sense, feel and bring us back to nature. The collection, meaning ‘The essences of Diptyque’ include five different perfumes, all incredibly different to suit each and every person. Lilyphéa, the poetry of plants, carries the scent of water Lillies. Rose Roche, shaped by the wings, carries the scent of the desert rose. Lunamaris, the reflection of moonlit water, carries the scent of the Mother of pearl. Coral Oscar, an unexpected contrast, carries the scent of coral. And lastly, Bois Corsé, a vital protective mantle, carries the scent of bark. These perfumes are the five treasures of nature.

Lilyphéa

Eau de parfum

Lilyphéa represents the vision of the water lily. With fresh notes of cardamom, subtle accords of violet leaves and a blend of sweet vanilla, Lilyphéa is created to perfectly capture the scent of the plant world. The name Lilyphéa, combines the French word “water lily” and “Nymphéas”, from the title of Claude Monet’s Water Lillies series.

Rose Roche

Eau de parfum

Rose Roche represents the vision of the desert rose. With fragrant notes of centifolia rose, subtle accords of lemon and a pinch of patchouli, Rose Roche is created to perfectly create the scent of the desert rose. The name Rose Roche plays on the words vegetable (Delicate) and the mineral (eternal).

Lunamaris

Eau de parfum

Lunamaris represents the vision of mother of pearl, and the moon reflecting off of the ocean water. With vibrant notes of pink peppercorn, subtle accords of incense, and amber rockrose, Lunamaris is created to perfectly capture the scent of mother of pearl. The name Lunamaris evokes the iridescence of the mother of pearl.

Coral Oscuro

Eau de parfum

Corail Oscuro represents the vision of the ocean’s coral. With strong notes of mandarin, warm accords of rose bourbon, and salty mineral accords, Corail Oscuro is created to perfectly capture the scent of coral. The name Corail Oscuro, plays on the words of the fiery red coral and the darkness of the sea bed.

Bois Corsé

Eau de parfum

Bois Corsé represents the vision of the tree bark. With strong notes of tonka bean, subtle accords of café arabica and a blend of sandalwood, Bois Corsé is created to perfectly capture the scent of tree bark. The name Bois Corsé plays on the French word “écorce”, meaning “bark”, and “corsé” meaning “full-bodied”.

Reconnecting with nature

Diptyque cannot simply reconnect us to our Mother Earth just through the scents of this collection, but also through sustainable practices. Through developing these captivating scents, depicting the 5 wonders of nature, we have exceeded European regulatory standards, the strictest in the world and have worked hard to create sustainable and safe perfumes that use raw and recycable materials, that truly give back to our Earth. This is the only way you can truly reconnect with nature through a perfume.

Crafted with elegance

Nathalie Cetto, a world renowned perfumer, created these wonderous scents, which are heavily inspired by nature to feed our creativity.

Collaborator Olivier Pescheux, worked on the initial scents and idea behind ‘Les Essences de Diptyque’ to create a collection that aligns with Diptyque’s values.

Les Essences de Diptyque represents the five treasures of nature, showcasing their true beauty.

Nigel Peake, a highly respected drawing artist, created the beautiful perfume bottle and its packaging, allowing each scent to vividly come alive.

These perfumes are inspired by nature in its rawest form to remind us and bring us back to what is really important.
